301 Service

只要將你的網域 CNAME 到 301.ronny.tw 或是 A 指到 52.69.187.52，並且加上 TXT record ，裡面寫入 "301 http://www.foo.com" ，就會自動將您的網址轉向到 http://www.foo.com
  • "301 http://www.foo.com/bar.html" 可以統一將所有存取都導到 bar.html 頁面
  • "301 http://www.foo.com/*" 後面加 * ，可以保留原來的網址導向
You can set the CNAME record to 301.ronny.tw with your domain, or set the A record to 52.69.187.52. And you can set TXT record to "301 http://www.foo.com", then your domain will be redirected to http://www.foo.com
  • "301 http://www.foo.com/bar.html" can redirect all request to /bar.html
  • "301 http://www.foo.com/*" add star symbol can preserved the request uri
